Important Legal Information

This Quarky App and website concerns youth-oriented problems. It is not intended to take the place of professional advice. The information in the app or on this website is intended to provide only support and general information. It is for information purposes only and offers you suggestions on where to get started when you need help. It should not be used or relied upon to replace counseling or therapy.
